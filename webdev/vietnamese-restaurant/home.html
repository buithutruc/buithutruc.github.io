About us

Little Vietnamese Restaurant was founded in 2022 by three close friends. Our story begins with the love of pho and Vietnamese food when we travelled to Vietnam. Back to home, even though there are plenty of pho restaurants where we are, none of them gives us the same flavor we tried in Vietnam, so we decided to open our very own Vietnamese restaurant!

We hope we add a unique and interesting place to our little town.

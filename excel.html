Data cleaning and dashboard creation in Excel

In this project we take raw bike buyers data, perform a cleaning process, then create a sales dashboard using Excel

Phase 1: Cleaning Data

  1. Copied the original, raw data to a working sheet
  2. Removed duplicates
  3. Made the data set look clearer, changing S/M in Marital Status to Single/Married, F/M in Gender to Female/Male
  4. Made sure the Income column was displayed as Currency
  5. Created a new column called Age Brackets to group people into their according age groups

Phase 2: Creating Pivot Tables

  1. A table showed the average income of people, breaking down by their gender, who bought/did not buy a bike
  2. A table showed the relationship between a person's commute distance and their decision of buying/not buying a bike
  3. A table showed the relationship between people's age brackets and their tendency of buying/not buying a bike

Phase 3: Creating Dashboard

The dashboard allowed viewers to see visually customers' tendency to buy/not buy a bike based on their marital status, region, education, and any other factor.
